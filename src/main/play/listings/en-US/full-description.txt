This application adds Longueuil RTL buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.rtl-longueuil.qc.ca, YouTube and @RTL_info and @RTL_Longueuil on Twitter.

RTL serves Longueuil, Saint-Lambert, Boucherville, Brossard and Saint-Bruno-de-Montarville.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport de Longueuil
http://www.rtl-longueuil.qc.ca/en-CA/open-data/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-longueuil-rtl-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport de Longueuil.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.rtl-longueuil.qc.ca, YouTube and Twitter
